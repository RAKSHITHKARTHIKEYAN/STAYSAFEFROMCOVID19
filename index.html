HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID-19

Made with love by ProGrad Junior,Rakshith.k

Wash Your Hands Regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for 30 seconds will help you avoid germs.

Cover Your Mouth And Nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or coughing in public. Even better use a mask.

Stay Home;Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or being in crowded place. Stay Indoor.

Eat Healthy

Avoid eating junk food. Eat as healthy as possible to boost your immunity.

Stay Calm

Regular excercise or meditation is a great way to keep yourself calm and composed.
